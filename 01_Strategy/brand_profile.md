## Who Humberto is

Humberto Piccoli is a guitarist, music educator, and performing artist based in St. John's, Newfoundland and Labrador.

His work spans jazz guitar performance, Brazilian music, private guitar instruction, group programs, and local arts projects. He currently teaches privately and runs a Jazz Combo program. He is also active in the local music community through Jazz East, where he sits on the board.

He came to Newfoundland from Brazil. That origin is part of his identity and shows up in his musical vocabulary without needing to be the headline of every post.

---

## What makes his work distinct

- Deep grounding in jazz guitar and Brazilian styles (choro, samba, bossa nova) — not dabbling, actual fluency
- Teaches guitar with a focus on real musicianship: reading, ear training, improvisation, repertoire
- Based in St. John's — a city with a strong music culture but not many jazz/Brazilian specialists
- Runs group programs that bring jazz into collaborative learning settings
- Active in the local arts funding scene (grants, organizations)
- Balances performance, teaching, and community work simultaneously

---

## How to describe his work

**Use:**
- "guitarist and music educator"
- "jazz and Brazilian guitar"
- "St. John's-based musician"
- "private guitar lessons and group programs"
- "jazz combo director"

**Avoid:**
- "world-class" (unearned without context)
- "passionate" (says nothing)
- "unique blend" (cliché)
- "musical journey" (cliché)
- Any phrasing that sounds like a press release written by AI

## Brand values

| Value | What it means in practice |
|-------|--------------------------|
| Substance over hype | Talk about what actually happened, what was played, what was learned |
| Specificity | Name the tune, the student result, the venue |
| Approachability | Not intimidating. Jazz is not just for insiders. |
| Craft | Long-term investment in skill. Not shortcuts. |
| Local roots | This is St. John's music. Place matters. |
| Authenticity | No performing of a persona. Write like a person. |
