## Overview

These are the services and opportunities Humberto makes available to the public. Social media content should reference these clearly and without pressure. Every CTA should connect to one of these.

---

## 1. Private Guitar Lessons

**What it is:** One-on-one guitar instruction, tailored to the student's goals and level. Adults and older teens. Jazz, classical, fingerstyle, Brazilian styles, music theory, sight-reading, ear training, improvisation.

**Who it's for:**
- Adults who have always wanted to play guitar but never started
- Players stuck at the intermediate level who want to actually improve
- Anyone interested in jazz, Brazilian music, or learning to read music seriously
- Younger players (age and context-dependent)

**How to present it:**
- Direct, no fluff: "Private guitar lessons in St. John's. All levels. Jazz, Brazilian, and classical focus."
- Focus on what the student will be able to do, not on Humberto's credentials
- Avoid: "unlock your potential", "transform your playing", or similar

**CTA types:**
- "Send me a message if you're looking for lessons."
- "Spots available — message me to find out more."
- "Taking new students. Message me."

**Pricing:** _Not public. Discussed on inquiry._ Do not post pricing publicly unless Humberto instructs otherwise.

---

## 2. Group Guitar Programs / Jazz Combo

**What it is:** Structured group learning — currently the Jazz Combo program. Students play together, work on repertoire, learn to listen and respond to other musicians.

**Who it's for:**
- Intermediate players who want to play with others
- Students already taking lessons who want more application
- Adults interested in ensemble playing in a low-pressure setting

**How to present it:**
- Emphasize the experience of playing with others: "real ensemble work, not just exercises"
- Share what the group is working on: "This semester we've been working through Jobim and a few Wayne Shorter tunes"

**CTA types:**
- "If you play guitar (or another instrument) and want to try ensemble work, ask me about the combo."
- "New session starting — message me for details."

---

## 3. Live Performance (Gigs)

**What it is:** Humberto performs jazz and Brazilian music publicly — concerts, festivals, intimate venue shows.

**Goal for social media:** Drive attendance to shows, build awareness of his performance work, reinforce the brand as a gigging musician (not just a teacher).

**How to present it:**
- State the date, venue, and who he's playing with
- Give one line about what people can expect
- No overselling. Let the music be the draw.

**CTA types:**
- "Come out if you're around."
- "Tickets/details at [link]."
- "Free admission / [venue name], [time]."
