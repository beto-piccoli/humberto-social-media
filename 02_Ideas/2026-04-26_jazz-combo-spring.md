## Core idea

The Jazz Combo is one of the most distinctive things Humberto offers. A group learning program where students play jazz together, work on repertoire, and develop ensemble skills. Sharing what the group is working on this spring serves multiple purposes: it documents the program, it's genuinely interesting to music fans, and it signals to potential students that this kind of collaborative learning is available.

## Angle suggestion

What the combo is working on this semester — specific tunes or concepts. What the group has accomplished since it started. The experience of ensemble playing vs. solo lessons. Inviting anyone interested in the next session.

## Key details to include

- What repertoire the combo is working on (specific titles)
- Something about the group's progress (without identifying individual students)
- Brief note on what ensemble playing offers that individual lessons don't
- Whether there are spots available in the next intake (confirm with Humberto before posting)

## PRIVACY RULE

No student names or identifying details. Group-level observations only.

## Possible CTA

Soft: "If you play guitar or another instrument and want to try ensemble work, ask me about the combo."

## Notes

Confirm with Humberto: what repertoire is the combo working on right now? What level are the students at? Is there a next session with open spots? This post should be timed when an enrollment window is opening or approaching.
