# Campaign Plan: Local Gigs — Spring 2026
_Slug: local-gigs-spring-2026_
_Owner: Nico_
_Created: 2026-04-10_
_Status: active_

---

## Objective

Build consistent awareness in St. John's that Humberto is a working jazz and Brazilian guitarist who performs locally. Drive attendance at upcoming shows.

## Success condition

- At least one gig post per gig gets solid organic reach (target: 200+ reach per post)
- Audience connects Humberto's name with live jazz/Brazilian performance in St. John's
- At least one inquiry or follow from a new audience member per show cycle

## Audience

Primarily Audience B (existing music community / St. John's locals) and Audience A (curious adults who might become students after seeing him perform). Secondarily Audience D (arts organizations).

## Platforms

Facebook (primary). Instagram (when active).

## Time window

- Start: 2026-04-10
- Duration: Rolling through the performance season (revisit at end of summer 2026)
- Peak: Around specific confirmed gig dates

---

## Message angles

1. **The show itself** — when, where, who's playing, what to expect. Simple and clear.
2. **What the music sounds like** — a note about the repertoire, the style, what the audience will hear. Specific.
3. **The musicians** — who Humberto is playing with (when applicable). Brief note on who they are.
4. **The experience of the gig** — a post-show recap: what happened, one specific moment.
5. **The venue / local context** — why this venue, why this night. Local color.

---

## CTA flow

| Post type | CTA |
|-----------|-----|
| Advance announcement (2 weeks out) | "Come out if you're around." / "Details in comments." |
| Close-in reminder (2–3 days out) | "[Venue], [day], [time]. No cover." / "Tickets at [link]." |
| Day-of | "Tonight — [venue]. Hope to see some of you." |
| Post-gig recap | No CTA — let the recap be the whole post |

## Content plan

| # | Pillar | Angle | Format | CTA type | Target timing | Brief |
|---|--------|-------|--------|----------|---------------|-------|
| 1 | Live Performance | Show announcement — next confirmed gig | Post + image | Soft invite | 2 weeks before gig | Date, venue, who's playing, one line about the music |
| 2 | Jazz & Brazilian Education | What we'll play — note about the repertoire | Post (text-heavy) | None | 1 week before gig | Specific tunes or style. Not a setlist — a direction. |
| 3 | Behind the Scenes | Rehearsal moment for this gig | Post + photo | Soft invite | 3–4 days before | Brief note on what the group has been working on |
| 4 | Live Performance | Day-of reminder | Post + image | Venue + time | Day of gig | Short. Date, venue, time. Warm tone. |
| 5 | Live Performance | Post-gig recap | Post (text) | None | Day after gig | One specific moment from the show. No hype. |

Repeat this 5-post cycle per confirmed gig. Gigs without full lead time can use a shorter version (2–3 posts).
