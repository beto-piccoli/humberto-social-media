# Campaign Plan: Personal Brand — Jazz and Brazilian Guitarist and Teacher
_Slug: personal-brand-2026_
_Owner: Nico_
_Created: 2026-04-10_
_Status: active_

---

## Objective

Establish Humberto Ramos as a known, credible, and distinct musician and educator in St. John's — and beyond — through consistent presence over time. This is not a short campaign. It is the ongoing foundation that makes everything else work better.

## Success condition

- Audience follows because of who Humberto is, not just because of event announcements
- Content is shared by other musicians and music fans because it's genuinely interesting
- When people in St. John's think of jazz guitar or Brazilian music teaching, Humberto comes to mind
- Portfolio of content that Humberto could show to grant committees, venue bookers, or arts organizations as evidence of a thoughtful, active artistic practice

## Audience

All four audience profiles. Personal brand content should speak broadly — but most strongly to Audiences B and C (music community + jazz/Brazilian enthusiasts). Audience D (arts organizations) benefits from the same content.

## Platforms

Facebook (primary). Instagram (planned). Both will benefit from this content type.

## Time window

- Start: 2026-04-10
- Duration: Ongoing — this campaign never ends; it evolves

---

## Message angles

1. **Jazz guitar perspective** — observations, recordings, approaches that Humberto actually thinks about
2. **Brazilian music and culture** — context for choro, samba, bossa nova that only someone who grew up with it can give
3. **St. John's context** — what it's like to play this music in Newfoundland. Local specificity.
4. **Musician's life** — honest reflection on the work, the practice, the uncertainty, the small victories
5. **The teacher and the student** — reflections on the teaching relationship and what music education actually does
6. **Influences** — named, specific, with real observations about why a particular musician or recording matters

---

## CTA flow

Personal brand posts rarely have hard CTAs. The CTA is the relationship itself.

| Post type | CTA |
|-----------|-----|
| Reflection / identity | None — let it be complete |
| Music education insight | None or very soft |
| Influence / recommendation | Soft: "Worth a listen." |
| Community / local | "Come out." (when there's an event) |
| Occasional bridge | "If you're interested in this kind of thing, I do teach lessons in St. John's." |
