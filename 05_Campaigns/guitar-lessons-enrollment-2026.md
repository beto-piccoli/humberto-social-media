# Campaign Plan: Private Guitar Lessons Enrollment
_Slug: guitar-lessons-enrollment-2026_
_Owner: Nico_
_Created: 2026-04-10_
_Status: active_

---

## Objective

Generate inquiries for private guitar lessons from adults in St. John's who are looking for a serious, approachable guitar teacher.

## Success condition

- At least 2–3 qualified lesson inquiries per enrollment push
- Builds an ongoing association: Humberto = serious guitar lessons in St. John's
- Attracts the right students (interested in jazz, Brazilian music, or real musicianship — not just "I want to play Wonderwall")

## Audience

Primarily Audience A — curious adult learners. People who have wanted to learn guitar, tried and stalled, or who are at an intermediate plateau. Also parents looking for a serious teacher for a motivated teenager (secondary).

## Platforms

Facebook (primary). Instagram (when active).

## Time window

- Start: 2026-04-10
- Ongoing (this is a year-round campaign with enrollment push intensity in:
  - September (fall enrollment)
  - January (new year motivation)
  - April (spring push — current)

---

## Message angles

1. **What lessons actually cover** — specific: reading, ear training, repertoire, improvisation. Not generic.
2. **Who lessons are for** — clear on the ideal student. Adults at any level. Jazz and Brazilian focus available.
3. **Student result moment** — anonymized, specific. Someone who achieved something real.
4. **The teaching approach** — why Humberto teaches what he teaches. What makes it different from playing random YouTube tutorials.
5. **Soft invitation** — "If you've been thinking about lessons, this is the time." No pressure. Direct.

---

## CTA flow

| Post type | CTA |
|-----------|-----|
| Awareness / teaching insight | None or very soft: "If you're curious about lessons, message me." |
| Student result | Soft: "Taking new students — message if interested." |
| Direct enrollment post | Direct: "Spots available for private guitar lessons. Send me a message." |

**Rule:** No more than 1 direct enrollment post per 2-week window during normal operations. During active enrollment push, up to 1 per week for 3 weeks, then back to soft CTAs.
