## Post copy (version 3)

Jazz East Big Band is at the Arts and Culture Centre on Saturday, April 18.

The programme is called Saltwater Songbook. The idea: take songs by some of Newfoundland's most well-known songwriters (Amelia Curran, Nico Paulo, Sean Panting, Jodee Richardson, Evan Smith, Valmy, Ana Luísa Ramos, Eastern Owl) and present them as new big band orchestrations. Several are world premieres.

Alongside that, the big band classics. Cole Porter, Luiz Bonfá, Tom Jobim.

Directed by Bill Brennan. And yours truly in the mix, alongside some of the finest musicians in this province.

One night only. Tickets at artsandculturecentre.com, link in the first comment.
