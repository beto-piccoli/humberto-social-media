## Post copy (version 3)

One of the pieces on Saturday's Jazz East programme is my arrangement of Nico Paulo's "Time," from her 2023 album.

I've been sitting with that song for a while. It's quiet. There's a lot of space in it, more than you'd expect. At some point I started wondering what would happen if you surrounded it with a full big band, and most of the work ended up being about when not to use them.

Nico Paulo has been rehearsing with us. Hearing her sing it with the band has been something.

I'm grateful for music that gives you that kind of problem to solve. Premieres Saturday at the Arts and Culture Centre. If you don't know the original: nicopaulo.bandcamp.com
